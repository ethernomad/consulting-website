Generative AI consulting focuses on large language models and related systems that can generate, summarize, search, classify, and reason over business information. This is currently one of the fastest-growing service lines for consulting firms.

Typical services in this area include:
- **Internal knowledge assistants** that help employees find answers from policies, SOPs, product documentation, and internal knowledge bases.
- **RAG systems over company documents** to provide grounded answers from enterprise content while reducing hallucinations.
- **Role-specific copilots** for sales, HR, legal, procurement, finance, or operations teams.
- **Content generation systems** for marketing drafts, product descriptions, email drafting, and social content support.
- **Enterprise AI search** across shared drives, cloud storage, help centers, and internal documentation.
- **Prompt engineering and evaluation frameworks** to improve reliability, consistency, and quality.
- **Fine-tuning or model customization** for organization-specific workflows, tone, or domain knowledge.

A mature generative AI offering also includes guardrails, access controls, evaluation, cost management, and human review workflows where needed.
