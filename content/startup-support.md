AI consulting firms can also serve startups, incubators, venture studios, and innovation teams within larger corporations. These clients often need speed, experimentation, and product thinking rather than large-scale enterprise transformation.

Typical services in this area include:
- **MVP development** for AI startups testing new products or business models.
- **Prototype and proof-of-concept builds** to validate demand before full investment.
- **Investor demo systems** that clearly show product vision and technical feasibility.
- **AI product discovery** including market research, user interviews, and prioritization of features.
- **Technical due diligence** for investors, acquirers, accelerators, or corporate innovation teams.
