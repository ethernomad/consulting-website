[[extra.faq_items]]
question = "How is Startup Support different from AI Strategy or App Building?"
answer = "AI Strategy is about deciding what to build and why — identifying opportunities and building a roadmap. App Building is about developing full-production cross-platform applications. Startup Support sits between them: it is the rapid validation and execution layer for early-stage ventures. We help you move from hypothesis to working prototype in weeks, with the explicit goal of getting to investment, first customers, or a go/pivot/kill decision. Many clients start with AI Strategy, move into Startup Support for rapid validation, and then graduate to App Building for production scale."

[[extra.faq_items]]
question = "How fast can you go from idea to working prototype?"
answer = "A focused prototype can be built in 2–4 weeks, depending on scope and data readiness. A complete MVP with a single core feature, user interface, and investor-ready demo typically takes 4–8 weeks. The key constraint is usually data — if your market or technology requires proprietary data that needs to be sourced or cleaned, that adds time. We start every engagement with a Discovery Sprint to scope the work accurately before committing to a timeline."

[[extra.faq_items]]
question = "What does a typical engagement cost?"
answer = "We structure engagements as fixed-scope sprints rather than open-ended time-and-materials. A Discovery Sprint starts from $5,000. A focused MVP with AI features and investor demo typically ranges from $25,000 to $80,000, depending on complexity and the number of iteration sprints required. We provide a fixed-price quote after the Discovery Sprint, so you know the full cost before committing to the build phase."
