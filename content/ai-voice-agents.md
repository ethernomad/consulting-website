AI voice agents use speech recognition, large language models, and natural-sounding speech synthesis to hold real-time conversations over the phone. They can answer inbound calls instantly, qualify leads, book appointments, handle support inquiries, and make outbound calls — all in natural, human-like conversation.

Businesses automating with AI voice agents see up to 90% reduction in operational costs versus traditional call centers, while never missing a call. The average business answers only 37.8% of incoming calls, and 85% of callers who reach voicemail never call back — making voice agent deployment one of the highest-ROI AI investments available today.

We help you choose the right approach — SaaS platforms for fast deployment, or open-source self-hosted solutions for full data control — and handle the full lifecycle from design to production.
