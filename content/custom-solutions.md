Custom AI development is for organizations that need solutions tailored to their workflows, industry, language, or internal systems. Rather than forcing businesses to adapt to generic tools, an AI consulting firm can design and build systems around specific needs.

Typical services in this area include:
- **Chatbots and virtual assistants** for customer support, sales, internal help desks, and employee self-service.
- **Document processing and OCR automation** for extracting information from invoices, contracts, forms, applications, receipts, and business records.
- **Recommendation engines** for e-commerce, media, education, and fintech products.
- **Forecasting and predictive analytics** for sales, inventory, staffing, maintenance, and demand planning.
- **Computer vision systems** for defect detection, ID verification, traffic analysis, retail shelf monitoring, and workplace safety.
- **Speech-to-text and voice AI** for call center analytics, transcription, voice bots, and multilingual customer interactions.
- **Retail and e-commerce**: product recommendations, customer segmentation, chatbot support, pricing analytics, inventory forecasting, and review sentiment analysis.
- **Banking and fintech**: fraud detection, KYC automation, document verification, chatbot support, credit scoring assistance, and collections optimization.

A consulting firm may handle the full lifecycle: problem scoping, data collection, prototyping, model selection, testing, integration, deployment, and post-launch support.
