A custom agent is an AI application purpose-built for your workflow — not a generic chatbot or a pre-packaged product, but a tailored agent system that speaks your domain language, connects to your tools, and executes the specific logic your business needs.

Unlike off-the-shelf AI products, a custom agent is built from the ground up to solve one thing well. It may be a voice agent that handles customer calls in Vietnamese, a coding assistant that knows your codebase's conventions, a RAG pipeline over your proprietary documents, or a multi-agent system that orchestrates research, drafting, and review.

We recommend ADK-Rust (by Google) and AutoAgents by LiquidOS — two production-grade open-source Rust frameworks for building custom agent applications. ADK-Rust excels at voice agents, agent-to-agent collaboration via the A2A protocol, and graph-based workflow orchestration. AutoAgents features WASM + OS-level multi-layer sandboxing, a CodeAct executor for sandboxed TypeScript execution, and native support for edge and embedded environments.

Both frameworks are provider-agnostic, memory-safe, and permissively licensed. Choose based on your use case — ADK-Rust for voice and streaming-heavy applications, AutoAgents for edge deployment and multi-layer security requirements.

Our team helps you go from concept to production: designing the agent architecture, building with your chosen framework, integrating your data sources and APIs, and deploying to your infrastructure.
