Background

Jonathan started his career building a complete multiplayer 2D game in C++ — implementing BSP trees, UDP networking with dead reckoning, and event-stream demo recording and playback (event sourcing before the term existed). This foundation in systems programming shaped his approach to software: rigorous, deterministic, and pragmatic.

Over the following decade he built and maintained enterprise Drupal platforms for AIG, NAB, and Flight Center — working with large SQL databases, complex migrations, and payment integrations. He contributed to Drupal Core, ran the Berlin Drupal community for three years, and spoke at events globally.

In 2015 he moved to Vietnam and transitioned into blockchain development. He built Acuity Index, a config-driven event indexer for Substrate blockchains, which received two Web3 Foundation grants and Kusama Treasury funding. The project features resumable indexing with span tracking, concurrent backfill and live-head catch-up with out-of-order resolution, and an embedded database — all in Rust.

Today Jonathan focuses on AI-augmented development, using agentic tools daily to deliver high-quality software faster. The throughline across his entire career is event-driven architecture, test-first engineering, and building systems that work reliably in production.

AI-Augmented Development

Jonathan was an early adopter of AI-agentic development tools. He uses Claude Code, Pi, OpenCode, and Hermes Agent for feature development, refactoring, and testing — and is building tai, a custom long-running agent in Rust. His approach emphasizes read-only planning, invariant enforcement via AGENTS.md files, and full test coverage across all projects. He has demonstrated that AI-augmented workflows can dramatically accelerate delivery velocity without sacrificing quality.

Personal

Jonathan has been a digital nomad in Vietnam since 2015, based in Da Nang. He is an async-first communicator comfortable working across EU and Southeast Asian time zones. He has explored insurance as a domain interest, including on-chain insurance DAO concepts. Outside of code, he enjoys cycling, yoga, swing dancing, and travel.
