Overview

What We Train

AI adoption doesn't stall because of technology. It stalls because people aren't prepared to use it consistently, confidently, and responsibly. We close that gap with hands-on training that connects AI capability to measurable business outcomes.

Our programs go beyond theory. Every session is built around your team's actual workflows, your data, and your technology stack. Participants leave with skills they can apply immediately — not completion certificates they will forget.

Role-based

Programs tailored to each audience — executives, engineers, operations, and compliance teams each get relevant, immediately useful content

Hands-on

Real exercises using your actual work scenarios, not generic demos. In-person and live virtual formats with direct instructor access

Outcome-driven

Every program defines clear success metrics tied to business goals — productivity gains, automation wins, and governance confidence

Programs

Our Training Programs

Six core programs covering the full spectrum of AI capability building — from the boardroom to the command line. Each is delivered in-person, live virtual, or as a multi-week cohort.

🎯

Executive AI Strategy & Leadership

Equip C-suite and senior leaders with the strategic frameworks to evaluate AI opportunities, govern risk, and lead enterprise-wide adoption — no technical background required.

  • AI opportunity identification and prioritisation
  • Organisational readiness assessment
  • Governance, risk, and responsible AI frameworks
  • AI-driven business model innovation
  • Leading through AI-enabled transformation
⚙️

Developer AI Engineering

Train your engineering teams to build production AI systems using modern tooling — from API integration and prompt chaining to custom agent development and model fine-tuning with Rust.

  • Building with AI APIs and function calling
  • Custom agent development (ADK-Rust)
  • Model fine-tuning with Burn
  • RAG pipelines and embedding strategies
  • Production deployment and observability
  • AI safety testing and red teaming fundamentals
🤖

No-Code AI Automation

Enable operations, marketing, sales, and support teams to build intelligent workflows and automate repetitive tasks using no-code AI platforms and agent builders.

  • Workflow automation with AI agents
  • Lead qualification and content generation
  • Customer support automation
  • Data extraction and report generation
  • Integration with existing business tools
💬

AI Literacy & Prompt Engineering

Build foundational AI competence across your organisation. Participants learn how AI works, how to craft effective prompts, and how to use AI tools safely and productively in daily work.

  • How AI models work — capabilities and limits
  • Prompt engineering patterns and techniques
  • Tool selection: which AI for which task
  • Secure and ethical AI usage habits
  • Building personal AI assistants
🛡️

AI Governance & Compliance

Prepare your legal, compliance, and risk teams for the evolving regulatory landscape. Covers the EU AI Act, GDPR requirements for AI systems, and practical governance frameworks.

  • EU AI Act risk classification and obligations
  • GDPR compliance for AI systems
  • AI charter and policy development
  • Bias detection and fairness auditing
  • Vendor risk assessment for AI tools
🏗️

Custom In-House Programs

A fully tailored curriculum designed around your organisation's technology stack, data environment, industry context, and strategic priorities. Built for enterprises with specific workflows, compliance requirements, or proprietary tools.

  • Curriculum designed around your stack
  • Training on your actual codebase and data
  • Train-the-trainer enablement packages
  • Post-program coaching and micro-learning
  • Measurable skill validation and certification
🏫

In-Person Bootcamp

2–5 day intensive sessions with direct instructor access, hands-on exercises, and cohort-based learning. Highest completion and skill retention rates. Ideal for foundational skill building.

  • Up to 40 participants
💻

Live Virtual Cohort

Multi-week programs delivered live online with structured sessions, assignments, and office hours. Combines flexibility with accountability. Best for distributed teams and ongoing learning.

  • 4–8 weekly sessions
🎯

Half-Day Workshops

Focused 3–4 hour sessions on a single topic — prompt engineering, AI governance, or tool-specific training. Ideal for leadership briefings, team kickoffs, or targeted upskilling.

  • Up to 15 participants
🔄

Ongoing Enablement

Post-training micro-learning, coaching calls, and reinforcement nudges delivered over 8–12 weeks. Ensures skills stick and adapt as tools and workflows evolve.

  • Weekly touchpoints
