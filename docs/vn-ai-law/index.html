VN AI Law Compliance

Vietnam's AI Law took effect March 2026. Compliance deadlines begin March 2027.

Book a consultation How we help

Key capabilities

  • • First AI law in Southeast Asia
  • • 35 articles, risk-based framework
  • • Applies to domestic and foreign entities
  • • Human-centric approach
  • • 12-month grace period
  • • Decree 142/2026 effective May 2026

Overview

What's in force and when

The law introduces obligations in phases. The compliance clock is running.

Most organisations have not yet conducted an AI inventory.
