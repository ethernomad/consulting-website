Startup & Innovation Support

Rapid AI prototyping, MVP development, and product validation for startups, incubators, venture studios, and corporate innovation teams — from idea to investor demo in weeks, not months.

How we help

Key capabilities

  • AI MVP development in 2–4 week focused sprints
  • Prototype and proof-of-concept builds for investor validation
  • Investor demo systems that showcase vision and technical feasibility
  • AI product discovery — market research, user interviews, feature prioritisation
  • Technical due diligence for investors, acquirers, and accelerators
  • Innovation lab setup and corporate venture building
  • Product-market fit testing and structured iteration frameworks
  • Full-stack Rust development for production-ready foundations

Overview

What is Startup Support?

Startup and innovation support is purpose-built for early-stage ventures and corporate innovation teams that need speed, experimentation, and product thinking — not large-scale enterprise transformation. Whether you are a first-time founder validating a thesis, an incubator preparing portfolio companies for demo day, or a corporate team launching a new venture, the goal is the same: get from idea to evidence as fast as possible.

We combine deep AI engineering in Rust with product discovery and lean startup methodologies. The result is a structured path from concept to funded, production-ready product — with working prototypes, real user feedback, and investor-grade demonstrations at every milestone.

Speed

Go from idea to working prototype in weeks, not months — with focused sprints that respect your runway and timeline

Validation

Test assumptions with real users and real data before committing to full-scale development or raising your next round

Scale

Build on production-grade Rust foundations that scale from MVP to million-user service without rewrites

Services

What We Offer

Every engagement is tailored to your stage, your market, and your timeline. These are the core capabilities we deliver for startups and innovation teams.

🚀

MVP Development

Build and ship AI-powered MVPs in 2–4 week focused sprints. We identify the single feature that proves your thesis and moves investors — then build it with production-grade Rust, not throwaway code. Your MVP is designed to be demoed, tested, and iterated, not discarded.

  • • Single-feature focus — one thing done well
  • • Production-grade Rust from day one
  • • Investor-ready demo included
  • • Iteration sprints post-launch
🔬

Prototype & Proof-of-Concept

Rapid prototype builds to validate demand, test technical feasibility, and demonstrate product vision — before committing budget to full-scale development. We focus on the riskiest assumptions first and produce working software, not slide decks.

  • • Technical feasibility validation
  • • User testing with real prototypes
  • • Architecture blueprint for scale
  • • Clear go / pivot / kill recommendation
📈

Investor Demo Systems

Polished, interactive demo systems built to win funding. We design every flow around your fundraising narrative — showing product vision, technical architecture, and market opportunity in a format that investors can explore and share with partners.

  • • Narrative-driven demo design
  • • Interactive — not slideware
  • • Ready for pitch meetings and demo days
  • • Technical architecture walkthrough included
📋

AI Product Discovery

Structured market research, user interviews, competitive analysis, and feature prioritisation to define what to build and why. We produce a prioritised roadmap with clear success criteria — reducing the risk of building something nobody needs.

  • • User research and pain point analysis
  • • Competitive landscape mapping
  • • Prioritised feature roadmap
  • • Success criteria and hypothesis definitions
🔍

Technical Due Diligence

Independent technical assessment for investors, acquirers, accelerators, and corporate innovation teams. We evaluate architecture quality, codebase health, team capability, technical debt, and security posture — delivering actionable findings and risk ratings.

  • • Architecture and code quality review
  • • Security and compliance assessment
  • • Team capability evaluation
  • • Risk-rated findings and remediation plan

Innovation Lab Setup

Design and operationalise innovation labs, venture studios, and corporate incubation programs. We help you establish the right processes, tooling, team structures, and governance models to consistently generate, evaluate, and launch new ventures.

  • • Idea intake and evaluation pipeline
  • • Venture governance and stage gates
  • • Cohort programme design and delivery
